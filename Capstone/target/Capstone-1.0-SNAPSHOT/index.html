About

who we are. Why we do it

The Technology Club is the point of convergence for a variety of technology focused disciplines at Valencia College. Though our focus is often on programming,networking,or general IT,we welcome anyone interested from any field of study.

Our goal is to build a community through which students can grow and prosper,learning from one another and through the club sponsored Tech Talks.

We host speakers from various industries,provide weekly study sessions for those who might need a little extra help,and are expanding into providing IT services for Valencia students at a significantly lower cost than might be found elsewhere.
